What Have We Accomplished?

The S&I Framework's committed participants have developed and defined an array of resources. These resources support the HIT community and their implementation of solutions that meet Meaningful Use objectives and other national healthcare priorities.

The S&I community has developed and defined an array of artifacts as part of the S&I initiatives, including:

  • Specifications
  • Implementation Guides
  • Information Models
  • Vocabulary and Value Sets
  • Test tools and Data
  • Reference Implementations

S&I Wiki includes all the current content and ongoing discussion being generated by the S&I Framework community.
S&I Repository Browser is a one-stop shop to search and download completed artifacts and other resources produced by the S&I Initiatives.
